About Me

Hi, I'm Nick Hayes. I'm a Rhodes Scholar at Oxford University, where I study the overlaps between mathematics, computer science, and linguistics, with a specialty with speech and language processing technologies.

In my free time, I enjoy hiking, rock-climbing, tennis, endurance sports, and learning new languages.
