Bio

I'm Nick Hayes, a graduate student studying Artificial Intelligence at the University of Oxford.


In particular, I focus on the overlaps between linguistics, mathematics, and computer science, with a specialty in speech and language processing technologies.


I love hiking, endurance sports, tennis, and chess.
